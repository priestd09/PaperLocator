Paperlocator is an easy and universal way to retrieve scientific papers from a given reference or citation.

It automates the process of extracting journal, issue and page from the text and searching the journal website manually by redirecting you to the correct page immediately.

Paste a piece of text containing a reference

Press enter or click one of the buttons
